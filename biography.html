Biography & Personal Life

Born in Boston, Massachussetts to actor David Poe Jr. and his wife Elizabeth Arnold Poe. He was orphaned in 1811 and therefore lived with his presumed godfather, John Allen. He attended the University of of Virginia, but later dropped out due to heavy gambling debt. Afterwards, Poe joined the United States military, during which he published his first work under a a false name. After leaving the military, Poe began to work for various literary jouranls as a critic, simultaneously publishing his own works. In 1845, Poe began to recieve notoriety for his poem "The Raven." Unfortunatly, he was unable to further his career, as he died shortly after, the cause of his death being unknown.
