Edgar Allan Poe

Welcome to all things Poe. Where we spotlight the father of the modern short story. A highly influential figure, Poe's collection of short stories and poems have inspired countless other novels and films. Even in his death, he remains a great deal of mystery. Everything to the inpirations behind his works to the cause of his death have remained unsolved, making him one of the most elusive figures in literature. Within this site, we will provide an autobiography as well as his most notable works.
